IBird and pigeon nets are specialized mesh barriers installed to prevent birds, particularly pigeons, from accessing certain areas such as balconies, windows, and rooftops.

Yes, bird and pigeon nets are designed to be humane deterrents that do not harm birds. The nets create a physical barrier without causing injury to the birds, allowing them to find alternative nesting and roosting locations. Can bird and pigeon nets be installed in any location.
